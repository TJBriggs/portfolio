GolfTEC is the world leader in golf instruction and lessons. We provide a comprehensive approach to game improvement with a complete arsenal of training tools to effectively make you the golfer you want to be!

We know that an on-going Game Plan will build your golf skills faster and provide more lasting results than an occasional golf lesson. The proof is in our 96% success rate.

Giving millions of golf lessons to thousands of golfers of every ability and seeing them improve, shaving strokes and enjoying the game more every day confirms our belief in a comprehensive approach to improvement.

At GolfTEC, you’ll have your own Certified Personal Coach who will get to know you and your golf game, and will guide you, motivate you and keep you focused and dedicated on meeting your goals of a better golf swing.

Training Bay

Here you will master the fundamentals of a solid, dependable swing that is easily repeatable on the golf course. We’ll sequentially address the key issues in your swing. Master your swing changes in a GolfTEC Training Bay and transfer better contact, more distance and greater accuracy to the golf course.

Outdoor / On Course

Translating what you learn in the Training Bay to the course is essential to improving your game, and with on-course lessons, your Coach is there to see how your swing is really working under the variable conditions on the course.

Putting

Grip, set-up, stance; we’ll address every part of the putting stroke. We'll work with different kinds of putting techniques to help you create a consistent swing line, contact and pace. You’ll learn putting drills you can take to the course to get you prepared for every round.

Short Game

This is where important skills are developed and better scoring happens. We’ll work on the different kinds of chipping and pitching shots that will let you stick a high approach shot, or master a chip and run, close to the pin. You’ll also learn to control your contact, spin, trajectory and roll.

A comprehensive Game Plan is the most effective way to build a solid, dependable swing that includes all the elements necessary for serious improvement. The Process of Improving Your Game

The 4 Factors of GolfTEC's Process
1Sequential Lessons

True improvement requires more than a quick-fix or sporadic lessons. Rather than overwhelm you with too many changes at once, GolfTEC focuses on a simple, step-by-step approach to ensure consistent improvement and performance. As each change is mastered, we build on that to achieve the best swing possible that can be comfortably repeated on the golf course. While it involves multiple lessons, it is actually a faster approach to creating results that last a lifetime.

2Advanced Retention Tools

To review and reinforce the improvement process, GolfTEC’s online Player Performance Center and GolfTEC TO GO app provide 24/7 access to all of your lessons, coach’s comments and recommended practice drills. You can view your lessons from your mobile device to help your progress on the range, the course or wherever you are. In addition, you can schedule future lessons and manage your GolfTEC account when it’s convenient for you.

3Video Practice

Practice between your lessons is imperative to lasting improvement. The interactive video in every GolfTEC hitting bay lets you see immediate replays of each swing from two directions simultaneously, making your practice sessions more productive and improvement even faster. You can even use our video drawing tools to add a swing plane, posture and other lines that make it easier to see when you are achieving your swing changes and practice goals.

4Fitted Clubs

The confidence of using properly fit equipment is another key to your golf success - independent of skill or handicap. GolfTEC’s TECfit club-fitting system provides a precise, unbiased fitting experience with our experienced, certified professionals. We will cross-reference your ball flight characteristics with a database of thousands of club and shaft combinations to find the models that will optimize the results of your swing. Clubs will then be ordered and custom-built to maximize your game, and at no more cost than off-the-shelf clubs.
